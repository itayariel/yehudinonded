About us

The goal of this site is to collect together all the Jewish sites that exists all over the world.
When religious people travel around the world, they have to consider where they will do Shabbat, eat kosher meals, pray, etc.
This site will gother all these places and all the information that exsists on these places.
In addition, it is possible to add new places to the site so that other people will be able to enjoy them as well
